The We-ness is a game I made with my friends one day, exploring language constraints. We spent an entire afternoon playing together, experiencing an almost psychadelic sensation as we combined our gathering with some simple language restrictions.

I adapted it into a gamescript submitted to Golden Cobra 2018, where it won a Judge's Choice.

If you're reading this, you probably saw the We-ness adapted to a simpler format for exploration in a convention or festival of some sort. Hello! You can read my full gamescript free at the Golden Cobra website, along with a bunch of amazing submissions from that year.

If you are coming at this as a social game without experience in the larp community, I highly recommend that you set clear boundaries of time (preferably a few hours) and space before playing, and start with some friends you trust. Furthermore, read up a little bit on larp community safety techniques, perhaps with one of Lizzie Stark's primers.
